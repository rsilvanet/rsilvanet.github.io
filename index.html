About me

I am a Software Engineer with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and over a decade of experience in software development. I have hands-on experience with distributed systems, message-based architectures, automated testing strategies, and cloud infrastructure and security design.

I like to focus on delivering reliable, maintainable systems and continuously improving engineering standards and processes. I am committed to ongoing learning and regularly adopt new tools, patterns, and practices to ensure the solutions I build remain scalable, secure, and aligned with modern software engineering principles.
